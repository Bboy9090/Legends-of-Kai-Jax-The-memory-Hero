GAME DESIGN DOCUMENT

Title: Super & Hyper Sonic Heroes: Kindness Quest
Platform: Mobile (iOS / Android)
Genre: Action–Adventure / Hero Brawler
Tone: Bright, fun, Nintendo–style charm with Sega speed & power.

⸻

1. Core Concept

Players guide Kai (Super Sonic Hero) and Jax (Hyper Sonic Hero) through a kindness-fueled quest. Instead of defeating enemies for domination, they convert negative energy into positive power, balancing combat and compassion.

⸻

2. Playable Heroes

Kai — Super Sonic Hero
	•	Role: Speed Leader
	•	Special: Kindness Dash (fast heal strike)
	•	Combo Style: Multi-hit spin chains
	•	Unique Passive: Gains boost when helping civilians

Jax — Hyper Sonic Hero
	•	Role: Power Striker
	•	Special: Brave Smash (high-damage finisher)
	•	Combo Style: Heavy shockwave punches
	•	Unique Passive: Builds shield meter when defending others

⸻

3. Combat System
	•	Basic Controls: Virtual joystick + 2 action buttons (Attack / Ability)
	•	Team Switch: Tap portrait to swap Kai ↔ Jax mid-battle
	•	Bond Meter: Builds as heroes aid NPCs or combo together
	•	Ultimate: “Kindness Wave” → wide AoE heal + damage

⸻

4. Game Loop
	1.	Enter a Zone (level).
	2.	Defeat “Unkindness Monsters” spawned from negative emotions.
	3.	Rescue NPCs, repair environments, spread kindness.
	4.	Build Bond Meter → unleash duo moves.
	5.	Defeat Boss → purify Zone.

⸻

5. World / Zones
	1.	Sunny Hillside Zone — Tutorial area.
	2.	Cyber City Zone — Enemies: Greed-Bots.
	3.	Crystal Cavern Zone — Enemies: Envy Serpents.
	4.	Storm Tower Zone — Enemies: Rage Titans.
	5.	Void Arena Zone — Final battle: Shadow of Cruelty (mega boss).

⸻

6. Enemy Design
	•	Greed-Bots: Hoard rings, weaken if ignored.
	•	Envy Serpents: Copy player attacks until defeated.
	•	Rage Titans: Slow, strong, but distracted by teamwork combos.
	•	Shadow of Cruelty: Boss with 3 phases (Darkness → Mimic → Despair).

⸻

7. Progression
	•	XP/Level Up: Unlocks new moves + passive boosts.
	•	Cosmetic Unlocks: Capes, gloves, insignias (via kindness missions).
	•	Bond Upgrades: New duo ultimates (e.g., “Brotherhood Beam”).

⸻

8. UI / UX
	•	Main Menu: Play / Heroes / Quests / Shop
	•	HUD: Health bar, Bond Meter, Switch button
	•	Kindness Counter: Tracks good deeds per stage

⸻

9. Monetization (Optional Hooks)
	•	Cosmetic Skins: Holiday outfits, glowing auras, alternate insignias.
	•	Energy System: Limited daily play → recharged by kindness tokens.
	•	Ad/Reward Loop: Watch ad = earn double kindness coins.
	•	Premium Pass: Early access to new zones, bonus skins.

⸻

10. Narrative Spine
	•	Heroes are chosen to protect the Kindness Cores.
	•	Each Zone is corrupted by an Unkindness Monster.
	•	NPCs teach values (sharing, patience, courage).
	•	Final battle restores harmony across worlds.

⸻

11. Art & Style
	•	Visuals: Bright, cel-shaded cartoon style.
	•	Character Art: Super/Hyper Sonic–inspired but unique brother designs.
	•	FX: Glows, spark trails, heart-shaped healing pulses.

⸻

12. Tech & Build
	•	Engine: Unity / Godot (mobile optimized).
	•	Assets Needed:
	•	2D sprite sheets (walk, run, jump, attack, ultimate).
	•	UI kit (buttons, menus, counters).
	•	Sound pack (Sega/Nintendo-inspired chiptune).

⸻

13. Future Expansions
	•	Co-Op Mode: Two players can control Kai & Jax simultaneously.
	•	Legacy Unlocks: New heroes (Nova Aran, Redlock, Kiro Kong).
	•	Tournament Arc: PvP kindness battles.
