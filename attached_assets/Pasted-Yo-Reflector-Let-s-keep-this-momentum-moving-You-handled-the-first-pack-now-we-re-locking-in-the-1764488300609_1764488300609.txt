### ⭐ **CHARACTER 8 — STAR SHAPER**
*(Kirby-equivalent – cosmic mimic)*

* **A) SAFE 3D MODEL OPTIONS**
    1.  **Quaternius – Ultimate Platformer Pack**
        * Look for the **"Slime"** or **"Round Critter"** model. It captures the shape without infringing on the face.
    2.  **KayKit – Spookies Pack**
        * The **"Ghost"** or **"Wisp"** models work great for a floating cosmic entity.
    3.  **Primitive Sphere (Fallback)**
        * A simple sphere with a custom shader (star texture) is actually a *strong* artistic choice here.

* **B) ART SPEC**
    * **Body Type:** Spherical or amorphous blob.
    * **Color Palette:** **Pastel Pink** (#FFB6C1) body, **Vibrant Azure** feet/nubbins.
    * **Texture:** Needs a subtle "stardust" shader—he shouldn't look like skin, he should look like condensed nebula.
    * **Eyes:** Large, innocent, but glowing with cosmic depth.
    * **Silhouette:** Round, low to ground, floats slightly.

* **C) LORE SHEET**
    * **Role:** The Wildcard.
    * **Origin:** Born from the fallout of a dying star.
    * **Personality:** Simple, hungry, infinitely powerful but emotionally simplistic.
    * **Quote:** *"Everything is fuel."*

* **D) GAMEPLAY KIT**
    * **Movement:** Float/Multi-jump (6 jumps).
    * **Combat:** Inhale (Vacuum), Star Spit (Projectile).
    * **Special:** **Mimicry** (Takes one special move from the last enemy eaten).
    * **Super:** **Singularity Event** (Black hole screen wipe).

* **E) TRANSFORMATION**
    * **Stellar Echo Mode:** Turns into pure light, body becomes translucent, infinite flight.

* **F) IMPORT NOTES**
    * `assets/models/star_shaper.glb`
