### ⭐ **CHARACTER 15 — KAI-JAX (MEMORY ASCENDANT)**
*(The Original Prodigy – 3-Tailed Hybrid)*

* **A) SAFE 3D MODEL OPTIONS**
    1.  **Quaternius – Sci-Fi Pack**
        * **"Cyborg Ninja"** or **"Advanced Soldier"**. You need a sleek, humanoid base.
    2.  **Mixamo – "Kachujin"**
        * A muscular, monstrous-but-heroic humanoid. Perfect for a powerful hybrid.
    * *CRITICAL:* You cannot find a model with 3 tails pre-made. You must use **Particle Effects** in Three.js (trails of Gold, Blue, White) to simulate the tails in-engine.

* **B) ART SPEC**
    * **Body Type:** Lean but muscular. Hybrid agility/power.
    * **Color Palette:** **White** base, **Gold/Blue/Purple** energy lines.
    * **VFX:** Three distinct energy trails (The Tails) constantly flowing behind him.
    * **Eyes:** One blue, one gold (Paradox eyes).

* **C) LORE SHEET**
    * **Role:** The Final Failsafe.
    * **Origin:** Born from the collective memory of erased heroes.
    * **Personality:** Conflicted, burdened, but infinitely determined.
    * **Quote:** *"I am the one who remembers."*

* **D) GAMEPLAY KIT**
    * **Movement:** Teleport-dash, wall run, hover.
    * **Combat:** Hybrid fighting style (uses punches, kicks, and energy blasts).
    * **Special:** **Memory Echo** (Rewind self 3s).
    * **Super:** **Tri-Aura Fusion** (Massive beam of all three colors).
