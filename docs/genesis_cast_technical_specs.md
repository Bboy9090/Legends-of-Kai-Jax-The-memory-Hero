### 1. KAI-JAX (The Memory King)
**Archetype:** Memory/Shape-shifter | **Role:** Protagonist  
**Weight Class:** 75 (Medium)  
**Unlock:** Story: Book 3, Chapter 5 (Post-Brother Convergence)

#### Visual Identity
- **Design Language:** Hard-Light Echoes (liquid ink tails that shimmer between forms)
- **Color Scheme:** Cyan-white primary, shadow-blue secondary
- **Silhouette:** Ethereal yet grounded, hints of both brothers' forms
- **Signature Effect:** Memory fragments orbit in spiral patterns

#### Core Mechanics
- **Passive: Empathetic Resonance** 
  - At 100% Resonance meter, can Shapeshift for 0.75s
  - During Shapeshift: Copy opponent's last move with 1.1x damage
  - Drains Resonance fully after effect ends

- **Signature Move #1: Flicker-Strike** (3f startup)
  - Quick combo initiator
  - Frame data: 3 startup, 4 active, 8 recovery
  - Damage: 3 (building block move)
  - Cancel point: Frame 5
  - Hits once; chains into follow-ups

- **Signature Move #2: Legacy Dash** (Quantum Tunnel)
  - Mid-range repositioning with invincibility
  - Movement distance: 2 screen units
  - Active frames: 1-frame invincibility window
  - Recovery: 6 frames
  - Cannot be input during startup/active of other moves

- **Signature Move #3: Sky-Anchor** (Bungee Recovery)
  - Ledge grab mechanic unique to Kai-Jax
  - Auto-grapples to ledge at 135° angle
  - Recovery frames: 12 (slower recovery than peers)
  - Can re-grab same ledge 3x before reset

- **Ultimate: Resonance Convergence**
  - Activation: 100% Resonance + SPECIAL input
  - Duration: 1.5 seconds
  - Effect: Channel both brothers' movesets simultaneously
  - Visual: Twin echo outlines

#### Stats

### 2. LUNARA SOLIS (The Wise Queen)
**Archetype:** Magic/Support | **Role:** Story Narrator & Player Guide  
**Weight Class:** 68 (Light)  
**Unlock:** Story: Book 1, Chapter 3 (First Gauntlet Entry)

#### Visual Identity
- **Design Language:** Moon Court aesthetic—silver robes with lunar crescent motifs
- **Color Scheme:** Silver-white primary, indigo secondary, moon-glow accents
- **Silhouette:** Regal, precise, otherworldly
- **Signature Effect:** Crescent moon trails on movement

#### Core Mechanics
- **Passive: Moon Core**
  - Team-wide Dread meter gain -30%
  - Solo play: Personal Dread decay +20% per second
  - Aura radius: Entire screen during Gauntlet battles

- **Signature Move #1: Hex-Magic** (6f startup, range-based)
  - Long-range projectile
  - Frame data: 6 startup, 3 active, 12 recovery
  - Damage: 4 per hit
  - Can hit 2x per activation if distance ≥ 1 screen unit
  - Recovery cancel: Frame 10

- **Signature Move #2: Phase-Step** (Teleport Dodge)
  - 4-frame invincibility escape
  - Distance: 0.8 screen units in any direction
  - Cooldown: 8 frames before next action
  - No attack during phase (pure repositioning)

- **Signature Move #3: Moon Blessing** (Support)
  - Heal self: +8% health
  - Range: Affects self + ally in radius
  - Duration: Instant
  - Cooldown: 120 frames (2 seconds)

- **Ultimate: Lunar Eclipse**
  - Activation: 100% Resonance + SPECIAL input
  - Duration: 2.0 seconds
  - Effect: Team invincibility + Dread meter freeze
  - Visual: Entire arena dims, moon shadows engulf all

#### Stats
```
Speed: 9/10

Range: 9/10
Weight: 4/10 (Very Light)
Combo Potential: 4/10
```

### 3. CHRONOS SERE (The Will of Tomorrow)
**Archetype:** Time/Utility | **Role:** Pattern Breaker  
**Weight Class:** 80 (Medium-Heavy)  
**Unlock:** Story: Book 2, Chapter 2 (Temporal Trials)

#### Visual Identity
- **Design Language:** Chrono-tech hybrid—clockwork armor fused with organic flesh
- **Color Scheme:** Gold-bronze primary, teal secondary, temporal distortion trails
- **Silhouette:** Imposing yet fluid, suggests both past and future
- **Signature Effect:** Clock hands orbit in expanding rings

#### Core Mechanics
- **Passive: Will of Tomorrow**
  - Once per 60 seconds: Bypass boss attack pattern entirely
  - Duration: 4 frames invincibility
  - Can be used reactively when enemy move enters active frames
  - No cooldown between uses if not triggered

- **Signature Move #1: Will-Blink** (Time Bypass)
  - Instant 4-frame invincibility skip
  - No startup, immediate active state
  - Recovery: 8 frames
  - Can chain into any move after active ends

- **Signature Move #2: Temporal Slash** (Delayed Hitbox)
  - Attack that hits 0.5s AFTER input
  - Allows for prediction and positioning
  - Frame data: 30 startup (delayed), 3 active, 6 recovery
  - Damage: 6 (higher power compensates for delay)
  - Unblockable during startup (opponent can't react in time)

- **Signature Move #3: Future-Sight** (Read Attack)
  - Predict opponent's next input
  - If prediction correct: +50% damage on next hit
  - If prediction wrong: -50% damage, +8 recovery
  - High risk/reward reads

- **Ultimate: Chronological Collapse**
  - Activation: 100% Resonance + SPECIAL input
  - Duration: 3.0 seconds
  - Effect: Time slows to 50% for opponent only (you move at normal speed)
  - Visual: Opponent surrounded by temporal stasis field

#### Stats

### 4. BORYX ZENITH (Guardian King)
**Archetype:** Heavy/Shield | **Role:** Powerhouse Protagonist  
**Weight Class:** 130 (Super-Heavy)  
**Unlock:** Story: Book 1, Chapter 1 (Opening Cinematic)

#### Visual Identity
- **Design Language:** Obsidian dragon-horns fused with weathered warrior armor
- **Color Scheme:** Black-obsidian primary, gold accents, crimson undertones
- **Silhouette:** Imposing statue—looks like a $200M museum piece
- **Signature Effect:** Gravity aura pulls at ground, cracks form with heavy attacks

#### Core Mechanics
- **Passive: Aero-Cape Flight**

  - While airborne: +20% horizontal movement speed

- **Signature Move #2: Shockwave Pound** (Heavy Finisher)
  - Full-screen push attack
  - Frame data: 8 startup, 5 active, 14 recovery

  - Knockback: Base +50% due to weight

- **Signature Move #3: Gravitic Pull** (Grab Variant)
  - Mid-range grab with vacuum effect

  - Frame data: 4 startup, 1 active, 8 recovery
  - Throw damage: 6
  - Throw direction: Toward center of arena

- **Ultimate: Sovereign Crown**
  - Activation: 100% Resonance + SPECIAL input
  - Duration: 2.0 seconds
  - Effect: +40% damage output, +20% defense
  - Visual: Crown of light materializes overhead

### 5. UMBRA-FLUX (Velocity Wraith)
**Archetype:** Speed/Momentum | **Role:** High-Execution Glass Cannon  
**Weight Class:** 58 (Very Light)  
**Unlock:** Gauntlet Mode: Complete 5 matches

#### Visual Identity
- **Design Language:** Matte-white form skating on light particles

- **Silhouette:** Sleek, unbothered, moves like liquid mercury
- **Signature Effect:** After-images trail behind every movement

#### Core Mechanics
- **Passive: Light-Skating**

  - Movement speed: +30% vs other characters
  - Momentum carries into attacks (fast moves feel faster)

- **Signature Move #1: Blink-Strike** (High Risk/Reward)
  - Teleport dash + auto-hit on land
  - Frame data: 2 startup, 3 active, 12 recovery if whiff

- **Signature Move #2: Velocity Slash** (Chain Move)

  - Each hit increases combo counter +1

  - Total 3-hit duration: ~27 frames

- **Signature Move #3: Void-Leap** (Recovery & Escape)
  - High-speed dash toward ledge
  - Movement distance: 1.5 screen units per activation
  - Can be used to escape combos (invincible during startup)

- **Ultimate: Sonic Convergence**

## GENESIS STORY MILESTONE: The Brothers' Sacrifice

### KAISON (The Heavy)
- **Solo Campaign:** "Bulwark" (Teach weight & impact)
- **Mechanic:** Shield-bash reflect
- **Arc End:** Holds mountain archway as Boryn turns back
- **Story Weight:** His sacrifice enables brothers' convergence

### JAXON (The Speed)  
- **Solo Campaign:** "Blink-Strike" (Teach risk/reward)
- **Mechanic:** High-execution teleport combo
- **Arc End:** Saved by Boryn mid-fall
- **Story Weight:** Speed without wisdom = death sentence lesson

Both brothers transcend into Kai-Jax's form post-genesis, leaving their movesets as accessible "echo" moves in his Shapeshift passive.

## BALANCE PHILOSOPHY

- **No Tier 0 Dominance:** Each character has clear strengths and matchup weaknesses
- **Execution Rewards:** Harder characters (Umbra, Chronos) deal more damage for precision input
- **Newcomer Gateway:** Boryx & Lunara have simple, forgiving movesets
- **Depth Scaling:** All characters support 200+ hour mastery curves

**The Genesis cast teaches the fundamentals. Future Eras will expand on these pillars.**
